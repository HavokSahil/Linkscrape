What does your website do?
Our website extracts and lists all the URLs present on a given webpage. It provides a comprehensive collection of links for easier reference and navigation.
+
How to use the platform?
Enter the website whcih you want to scrape and click the scrape button. Wait and see the MAGIC!
+
Can I scrape multiple URLs simultaneously?
No you can't scrape multiple URLs simultaneously, but you can scrape them one by one. All of the scraped links will be collected.
+
Are there any restrictions on the types of websites your tool can scrape?
Our tool can scrape URLs from most public websites. However, certain websites with strict security measures or complex dynamic content might pose challenges in extracting links.
